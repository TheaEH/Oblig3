About buses

Buses have long been a part of the busy day to day life of many people. Some take the bus to get to work, others to school. Many countries even have school buses arranged to pick children up so that they can get to school.
In developing countries, there is a lack of funding for school. They might not be able to afford electric charging stations stations, and normal buses pollute the air. However, there exists buses with solar panels on the roof, which allows them to recharge everytime the sun shines!
Now If one were to place out such buses in different regents of developing countries, they could be used to transport children to and from school, and aid them in getting an education.
